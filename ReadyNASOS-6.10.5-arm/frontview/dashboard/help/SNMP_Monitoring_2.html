Download the NETGEAR SNMP MIB

You can download the NETGEAR SNMP MIB from the local admin page and import it to your SNMP client applications. For information about the types of messages that the ReadyNAS can send to SNMP hosts, see System Logs.

To download the NETGEAR SNMP MIB:

  1. Log in to your ReadyNAS.
  2. Select System > Settings > Services.
  3. Click the SNMP button.
  4. Click the Download MIB link.

    The page updates showing the MIB.
